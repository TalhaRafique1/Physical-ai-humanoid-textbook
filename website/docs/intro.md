Welcome to the AI-Native Interactive Textbook on Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics! This textbook leverages cutting-edge AI technology to provide an engaging and personalized learning experience.

- **AI-Powered Assistance**: Get instant answers to your questions via our RAG chatbot

## Course Objectives

By the end of this course, you will be able to:

- Understand the fundamentals of Physical AI and its applications
- Explain the principles of humanoid robotics
- Analyze sensor systems and perception in physical AI
- Evaluate motion planning and control systems
- Assess ethical considerations in humanoid robotics
- Apply concepts to real-world scenarios

## Target Audience

This textbook is designed for undergraduate and graduate students, researchers, and professionals interested in robotics, AI, and physical computing. Some familiarity with basic programming and mathematics is helpful but not required.

## Prerequisites

While we strive to make this content accessible to learners at different levels, the following background knowledge will be helpful:

- Basic understanding of programming concepts
- Fundamental mathematics (algebra, basic calculus)
- Interest in robotics and AI
